FAQ

What is a Make-A-Thon?

A Make-A-Thon is a gathering like-minded and passionate students to make their ideas into a reality. Learn new things, ask questions, discover something new, and have fun!

Who can attend?

Any high school student in North NJ or Greater New York City area is free to partipate.

What if I'm a beginner?

Don't worry! We encourage beginners to embark on their making journey. Be ready to learn a ton from mentors and have a lot of fun with your teammates!

What should I bring?

You should bring your materials for your project, laptop, chargers, phone, and most importantly, ideas! All food, drinks, and snacks will be taken care of.
