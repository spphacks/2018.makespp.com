MakeSPP is a day-long Make-A-Thon that will bring over 100 innovative students to build the future of science, engineering, and technology. Make cool projects, meet other passionate students, and learn alongside amazing mentors.


If you're planning on coding an app for the first time or an experienced maker designing a world-changing science experiment, join us for an awesome day of making!
